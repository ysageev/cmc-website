# "Do"s and "Don't"s

## "Do"s of Video Surveyors

|   |                                                                                                    |
| :--------  | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Follow all OSHA guidelines when on a project site. (No Exceptions)|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Plan visits in an efficient manner of travel. (Schedule the most visits possible in a day with efficient travel route.)|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Only correspond with Primary/On-Site Contact to inform them of your arrival date/time for the site capture visit.|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Always arrive within 15 minutes of the expected arrival time of site capture.|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Inform the onsite contact if you are running later than the expected arrival time.|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Be curteous, respectful, and behave in a professional manner when onsite as you are a representativeof the company.|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Follow any check in/out procedures that are required by the contact on their project site (Signing In, visitors log, etc...)|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Use best judgement regarding safety and accessibility. (Refer to OSHA Guidelines)|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Always Format the SD Card prior to any Site Capture. This limits the potential for files to become corrupt when backing up on to computer.|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Check the video files in GoPro menu after Site Capture to ensure the files were captured correctly.|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Perform a walkthrough with the onsite person when units are occupied by tenants.|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Provide legible “Date” and “Project Title” for SD Card labels before mailing them back to the main office.|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Provide detailed information in surveyor notes if any deviations from the regular site visit have been made. (ex. couldn’t access 3rd floor due to abatement)|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Operate, manage, and maintain your Video Surveyor Equipment Kit.|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Keep the Equipment in prime condition by performing regular maintenance and cleaning. Store in a cool, dry, and secure place.|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Notify the Consultant if equipment is faulty or needs to be replaced.|
| <span style={{color: "green", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Do</span> |Narrate during shooting to provide better information to the consultant when necessary. (This helps ensure all areas are captured and consultant knows the path of travel)|



## "Don't"s of Video Surveyors


|   |                                                                                                    |
| :--------  | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| <span style={{color: "tomato", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Don't</span> |Arrive to a project under the influence of any drugs, alcohol, and/or other illegal substances that result in impaired judgement.|
| <span style={{color: "tomato", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Don't</span> |Visit sites outside of business / operating hours.  [Monday-Friday, 7:00am-3:30pm]|
| <span style={{color: "tomato", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Don't</span> |Start visit too early / late. Natural Light is important for recording purposes.|
| <span style={{color: "tomato", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Don't</span> |Enter occupant units without the On-Site/Primary Contact or authorized person.|
| <span style={{color: "tomato", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Don't</span> |Capture video of tenants in occupied units for housing projects.|
| <span style={{color: "tomato", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Don't</span> |Share any information that is attached/included in email correspondence that doesn’t relate to job duties of capturing the site.|
| <span style={{color: "tomato", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Don't</span> |Use the equipment for any activities outside of your job responsibilities as a video surveyor or outside of the company.|
| <span style={{color: "tomato", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Don't</span> |Let anyone other than yourself borrow, operate, or handle the video surveyor equipment.|
| <span style={{color: "tomato", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Don't</span> |Keep equipment stored in extreme hot/cold temperature areas or in places easily accessible by other people.|
